About the Project


Hello my name is Aakash.I live in Kharghar,Navi Mumbai. In my locality I always see that for checking any meter, be it Water meter or Electricity meter, Government here sends a guy to click a photo of the meter and then the bill comes to us by a guy.

So Basically,I am making a Web Application to remove the use of human-effort and let machine grasp the reading and send it to the website. After recieving the reading, we can display the calculated bill amount.
